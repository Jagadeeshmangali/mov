Top 5 Telugu movies of all time.

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)

Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy Description: This epic Indian historical fantasy film is the sequel to "Baahubali: The Beginning." It follows the story of Shivudu (Prabhas), who discovers his true identity as Mahendra Baahubali, the rightful heir to the kingdom of Mahishmati. He must confront his evil uncle Bhallaladeva (Rana Daggubati) and reclaim his throne. The film is known for its grand visuals, action sequences, and emotional drama.

RRR (2022)

Genre: Action, Drama, Historical Fiction Description: This Telugu-language period drama is a fictionalized account of the lives of two real-life Indian revolutionaries, Komaram Bheem (N. T. Rama Rao Jr.) and Alluri Sitarama Raju (Ram Charan). Set in the pre-independence era, the film explores their friendship and their fight against British colonial rule. RRR is praised for its high-octane action sequences, performances, and powerful themes.

Kantara (2022)

Genre: Action, Drama, Thriller Description: This Kannada-language film is set in a coastal village in Karnataka, India. It explores the conflict between humans and nature, specifically focusing on the relationship between the villagers and a mysterious forest deity. Rishab Shetty writes, directs, and stars in the film, delivering a captivating performance. Kantara has received critical acclaim for its storytelling, performances, and exploration of folklore and environmental themes.

Animal (2023)

Genre: Action, Crime, Thriller Description: Directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, this upcoming Hindi-language film stars Ranbir Kapoor and Rashmika Mandanna in lead roles. Details about the plot are still under wraps, but based on the teaser trailer, it appears to be a high-octane action thriller. The film is scheduled to release in August 2023 and has generated significant buzz due to its star cast and director.

Maharshi (2019)

Genre: Drama Description: This Telugu-language film is a coming-of-age story that follows Rishi Kumar (Mahesh Babu), a businessman who grapples with his ambitions and ideals. The film explores themes of success, family, and finding your purpose in life. Maharshi received positive reviews for its performances, emotional story, and exploration of contemporary societal issues.
